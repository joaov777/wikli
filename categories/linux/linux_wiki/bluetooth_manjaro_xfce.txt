### ENABLING AUTO CONNECTION

- Automatically switch to newly-connected devices
sudo vim /etc/pulse/default.pa
load-module module-switch-on-connect

bluetoothctl
trust [DEVICE_MAC]

vim /etc/bluetooth/main.conf
Add the following lines:
[Policy]
AutoEnable=true
